  • About Me

    I’m Ethan Woronko, a designer and illustrator seeking to push my creative boundaries and experiment as much as I can. Due to my experimentation, I tend to be a bit eclectic and collect new ways to render an illustration or approach a design problem. I stay constantly curious and love learning new things and facts while working on design projects and digging to find what makes a product or concept special. No matter the project, I am enthusiastic and determined to make it the best it can be and will rework something till its perfect.
